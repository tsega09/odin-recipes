CHECHEBISA

Chechebsa, also known as Kita Fir Fir in Ethiopia, is a traditional breakfast dish that commences with the preparation of a batter, fried to create a sizable, thin flatbread known as Kita. Subsequently, the Kita is torn into small fragments and combined with berbere and Niter Kibbeh spiced butter until it forms a moist and tender mixture. Typically enjoyed warm, Chechebsa is often accompanied by hot coffee or chai spiced tea. This rendition focuses on serving the dish warm, providing a choice between enjoying it as is, akin to a savory bread pudding, or enhancing its flavor profile by incorporating cooked vegetables or other ingredients for a delightful culinary experience.
